About Stillness

A contemplative journey created with generative music and soothing visual meditation, promoting relaxation, focus, stillness and presence.

The Four Boats:
Each boat represents a musical mode, a different emotional landscape. Major (bottom left) and Lydian (top left) are bright and hopeful. Aeolian (top right) and Dorian (bottom right) are introspective and deep.

The Moon:
Touch it to begin. Touch again to pause. The golden glow means music is flowing.

For Sleep:
Choose Aeolian or Dorian. Let the waves carry you.

Made with love for those seeking stillness.
